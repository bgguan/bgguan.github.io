Why Audiobooks are Great (and More)

Written by Brian; Not yet published

Books. In the twenty-first century, we are finding more and more ways to consume stories and information. There are devices now that allow you to read a book with a click of a button. But many people don't use a valuable resource: audio. Here is why audiobooks are amazing, with a few titles for you to enjoy.



Reason #1: It's Convenient

via GIPHY

When you're doing chores, or you're brushing your teeth, do you have time to pull a book out of your local library? No! That's where audiobooks come in. Just pop in your earbuds, and enjoy one of your favorite stories. Now, you can access your books just by pressing play. Next time you're doing something that's boring (besides homework), and you don't want to listen to homework, listen to your favorite audiobook.

Reason #2 - It's Good for Car Rides

via GIPHY

Ever been bored on a long car ride? Do you get headaches when reading on the car? Well, solve that problem with audiobooks. Just pop in your earbuds, or insert the disk into the car, and listen to a good story on the car. Bonus: You probably won't get a headache!

Reason #3 - It Can Be More Immersive

via GIPHY

Love how movies can make you feel like you are actually there? That is kind of the sensation that you get when listening to a good audiobook. Many audiobooks are performed by gifted actors, which can make you feel like you are listening to someone tell you a story. Immerse yourself in one of your favorite stories that's narrated by a great actor
